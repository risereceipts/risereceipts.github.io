# Receipt Inbox

A no-frills web app: snap a receipt photo, email it to an inbox of your choosing.

## How it works

1. Open https://ultrowillyhub.github.io/receipt-inbox-web/ on your iPhone Safari
2. Tap the share icon → **Add to Home Screen** (gives you an app icon)
3. Open the app from the home screen
4. Tap ⚙ to set your destination email (e.g., `receipts@yourdomain.com`)
5. Tap **Capture** → take a photo → iOS share sheet opens
6. Pick **Mail** → email is pre-composed with the photo attached → tap **Send**

The app stores nothing on a server. Your destination email is saved locally on your device.
Photos are never uploaded anywhere by Receipt Inbox itself — they go through the Mail app
on your device using whichever email account you've set up in iOS Mail.
