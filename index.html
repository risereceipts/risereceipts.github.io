Receipt Inbox

Snap a receipt and email it to any inbox you choose.

Tap Capture, take a photo, then send via Mail.

Settings

Send Receipts To

When you tap Capture, this is the email address that gets pre-filled in the Mail composer. Leave blank to type the destination yourself each time.

Your Name

If set, "Captured by [Your Name]" is added to the email body so the recipient knows who sent it.
